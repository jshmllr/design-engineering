# Week 1: Foundations — Role, scope, and mindset

## Objectives
- Define what design engineering is and isn’t.
- Understand where design engineers operate within teams.
- Identify common deliverables and organizational impact.

## Daily plan
**Day 1 — Definitions and origins**
- Read the foundational sources on the origins and definitions of design engineering.
- Draft a 1–2 paragraph definition in your own words.

**Day 2 — Team collaboration**
- Map how design engineering collaborates with product design, engineering, and product management.
- Create a simple team diagram.

**Day 3 — Deliverables**
- List 6–8 deliverables a design engineer might own (prototypes, motion specs, UI tooling, component libraries, etc.).
- Note the intended audience for each deliverable.

**Day 4 — Attributes and skills**
- Identify attributes or behaviors emphasized in the readings.
- Write a short skills gap analysis (what you have vs. what you need to build).

**Day 5 — Weekly summary**
- Write “What design engineering is and isn’t” as a short reflection.

## Outputs
- Definition draft
- Team diagram
- Deliverables list
- Skills gap analysis
- Weekly summary
